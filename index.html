Controls:
+,- - inc/dec simulation speed
r,f - increase/decrease distance to center
. - switch helio/geocentric
p - pause simulation m - show/hide comets/asteriods
n - show/hide names of bodies
w,s - rotate X axis
a,d - rotate Y axis
q,e - rotate Z axis
b - show/hide skybox
l - toggle lines
